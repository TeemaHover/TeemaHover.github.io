Cowboy Ventures

Cowboy Ventures is a seed-stage focused fund investing in digital startups based in Palo Alto, CA. They work with founders who are building products that “re-imagine” work and personal life in large and growing markets – an area that they call “Life 2.0″. Led by Aileen Lee and Ted Wang, Cowboy Ventures previously invested in many successful companies including Product Hunt, Dollar Shave Club, Polar, and DocSend. Цааш судлах
